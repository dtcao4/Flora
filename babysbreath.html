03. BABY'S BREATHS

BABY'S BREATH

Gypsophila

In early summer, gardens are brought to life as clouds of delicate and lacy baby’s breath flowers bloom until the onset of autumn. Baby’s breath comes in different colors: white, shades of pink, purple and yellow. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, baby’s breath is also found across North America and other parts of the world. This flower is symbolic of the love and emotion that people share, and reminds us to cherish and show affection to the ones you love. Baby’s breath also symbolizes a past love that is now lost, yet still cherished.
