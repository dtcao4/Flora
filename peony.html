02. PEONIES

PEONY

Paeonia

Known as “the floral symbol of China, the state flower of Indiana, and the 12th anniversary flower,” this versatile perennial blossoms into lush flowers between late spring and early summer. Peonies embody romance, compassion, luck, and prosperity. With their lush, full, rounded bloom, and their long lifespan (that is, if they are properly tended to), peonies embody romance and are gifted with wishes of good fortune and a happy, long-lasting marriage filled with love.
