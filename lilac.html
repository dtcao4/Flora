06. LILACS

LILAC

Syringa Vulgaris

After the gloomy winter, lilacs welcome in the spring with their flowers and sweet fragrance. Native to Eastern Europe and some regions in Asia with cool summers, lilac is the flowering shrub from the Syringa genus of the Oleaceae family. The Lilac flower meaning is symbolic of the joy of youth as lilacs bloom in spring. In general, it symbolizes a fresh start, renewal, and innocence, and in romance, the flower stands for confidence and bygone love. Usually, lilacs are sent to express love to someone, typically a first love.
