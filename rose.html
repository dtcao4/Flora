01. ROSES

ROSE

Rosa Rubiginosa

A timeless and beautiful flower, the rose is a symbol of some very powerful emotions. From love, passion, and admiration, roses are the perfect embodiment of strong feelings and desires for your special person. This popular flower has held such meaning for years. With its unique combination of thorny stems and fragrant blossoms, roses are also a symbol of achievement, completion, and perfection. Often, roses are used romantically to communicate “I love you” or “I want you” in strong passion and intimacy.
