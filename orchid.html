04. ORCHIDS

ORCHID

Orchidaceae

One of the major orchid genera is Paphiopedilum. The name is derived from the word “Paphos” which is the name of the temple where the Greek goddess of love, Aphrodite, was worshiped. This flower has always been associated with fertility, love, and passion. Due to its exotic look, orchids give any place a romantic feeling. This, in association to nobility and respect, orchids require special attention and love. On some occasions, these flowers were used to represent virility. Still, orchids have been a gift of love, as they symbolize our desire to reach greatness or to develop intimacy.
