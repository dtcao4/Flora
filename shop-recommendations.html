KEEP YOUR OPTIONS OPEN

It’s easy to find flowers at your local stores, but if you can’t make the trip, then check out these online flower bouquets! It’s not “being lazy” at all if you are considerate of the overall bouquet.

BOUQS.COM

Bouqs is a wonderful online flower shop! They partner with sustainable farms that recycle water and minimize waste. They also offer next day flower delivery!

website here

FTD.COM

Another great online flower shop is the Florists’ Transworld Delivery (FTD). They also have excellent delivery options and beautiful bouquets!

website here

AFLORAL.COM

Unless potted in soil, flowers will eventually die when cut from their stem. In that case, artificial flowers are an alternative. Afloral is a great company for artifical bouquets and plants!

*Please note that Afloral does not offer bouquet arrangements.
